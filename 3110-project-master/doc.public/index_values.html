Index of values

A
add [Matrix]

add m1 m2 is the sum of matrices m1 and m2.

I
id [Matrix]

id n is the nxn identity matrix.

M
make [Analytic]

make f is the t representation of an f_expr f.

mult [Matrix]

mult m1 m2 is the matrix product of m1 and m2.

R
rand [Matrix]

rand i n m is the random nxm matrix with ints from 0 to i.

root [Analytic]

root e t is a root of t up to e digits.

S
scale [Matrix]

scale r m1 is the scalar multiple of m1 by r.

subtract [Matrix]

subtract m1 m2 is the difference of matrices m1 and m2.

T
transpose [Matrix]

transpose m1 is the transpose of m1.
